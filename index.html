Connect your Sheet

This app saves your sales to your own Google Sheet via a small Google Script — so your data stays in your Drive, fully separate from anyone else's.

Open the template script ↗
  1. Click the copy icon to copy all the code
  2. Open script.google.com ↗On mobile: tap your browser's menu (Safari: tap aA in the address bar / Chrome: tap ⋮) → Request Desktop Site
  3. Click New project — then click "Untitled project" at the top to rename it (e.g. Wolf's Cashier)
  4. Delete the starter code and paste in the copied code
  5. Click the ▼ arrow next to Deploy → New deployment → Web app
  6. Set "Who has access" to Anyone → click Deploy
  7. Google will ask you to authorise access — click Authorize access → if warned "app isn't verified", click Advanced → Go to [name] (unsafe) → Allow. This is safe — it's your own script.
  8. Copy the resulting URL (ends in /exec) and paste it into the field belowIf a pop-up says "Sorry, unable to open file" — close it, then click Deploy → Manage deployments to find your URL.
